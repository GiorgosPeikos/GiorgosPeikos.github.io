<div class="card section">
  <h1>Vintage Retail: Applied Case Study (Hobby)</h1>

  <p class="muted">
    Vintage Retail is a hobby retail venture that I run on my free time.
    I am genuinely interested in vintage and antique objects, especially the uniqueness of items that were handmade and tailored to their future user.
  </p>

  <p class="muted">
    I also use the venture as a practical case study to apply my technical expertise and develop new skills.
    I build small applications and lightweight automation to support recurring tasks in listing, pricing, promotion, and workflow tracking.
  </p>

  <p class="muted">
    The project helps me develop skills that are less central in my current professional role, including unit economics, marketing experiments, inventory management, and operational decision-making.
    It is also a useful contrast with my computer science background, because it forces prioritisation of simple tools that deliver value, while keeping feature complexity under control.
  </p>

<div class="card section">
  <h2>Why it matters professionally</h2>
  <p class="muted">
    Vintage Retail is a practical setting where I act as both the system designer and the end user of my own tools.
    It strengthens my ability to translate AI methods into simple workflows with clear business metrics, real constraints, and fast feedback.
    It also highlights a recurring engineering trade off: building only the features that create measurable value, rather than over engineering.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="card section">
  <h2>What I do</h2>

  <div class="project-grid">

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="item-title">Sourcing and opportunity discovery</div>
      <div class="muted small">
        I perform product search to identify items with resale potential before visiting flea markets and online listings. I focus on signals such as demand, rarity, and comparable sold prices.
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="item-title">Negotiation and real time deal evaluation</div>
      <div class="muted small">
        I negotiate deals in flea markets and evaluate profitability fast, considering platform fees and expected resale price.
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="item-title">Post purchase research and listing creation</div>
      <div class="muted small">
        After buying, I analyse item history, details, and intended use, then create listings for online marketplaces such as eBay.
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="item-title">Distribution and community based promotion</div>
      <div class="muted small">
        I identify where potential buyers are active across platforms and communities, and adapt product communication to match channel and audience expectations.
      </div>
    </div>

  </div>
</div>
